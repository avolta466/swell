# Vibe Coding in Utah
### A Short Documentary — 10 Minutes

---

## OPEN (0:00–1:00)

*EXT. UTAH DESERT — DAWN*

Wide shot. Red rock formations catching first light. Silence except for wind.

Cut to a distant highway. One car. Moving slow.

*INT. HOME OFFICE — CONTINUOUS*

Close on a keyboard. Hands hover, then begin to type.

The monitor glow reflects in glasses.

A terminal blinks.

**TITLE CARD:** *Vibe Coding in Utah*

---

## MEET THE CODERS (1:00–4:00)

*INT. BASEMENT OFFICE — PROVO*

Subject 1 at their desk. Two monitors, a half-eaten granola bar, a window showing a sliver of mountain.

> **SUBJECT 1 (V.O.)**
> "I moved here for the quiet. There's something about not being in a city that makes it easier to think."

Cut to their screen — an AI chat interface, a code editor, switching back and forth.

---

*INT. COFFEE SHOP — SALT LAKE CITY*

Subject 2 with laptop at a corner table. Airpods in. Barely notices the room around them.

> **SUBJECT 2 (V.O.)**
> "I don't really plan what I'm building. I just... start. The AI fills in the gaps. I steer."

Close on their fingers. The screen scrolling fast.

---

*INT. CONVERTED GARAGE — MOAB*

Subject 3. Standing desk. The garage door is open — red canyon visible behind them.

> **SUBJECT 3 (V.O.)**
> "Silicon Slopes is like an hour away but it feels like another world from here. I like that."

---

## THE VIBE (4:00–7:00)

*No dialogue. Ambient soundtrack begins.*

A sequence of quiet moments:

- Cursor blinking in an empty file
- A prompt typed into an AI tool. A response streams in.
- Coffee steam rising beside a trackpad
- Terminal output scrolling — green on black
- A window resize. A browser refresh.
- Outside: clouds moving over a ridge
- Back to the keyboard. Small smile.
- A commit pushed. Status: success.

*The music swells slightly, then settles.*

---

## REFLECTION (7:00–9:00)

*Back to each subject. Still at their desks. Evening light now.*

> **SUBJECT 1 (V.O.)**
> "I used to fight the code. Now I kind of... collaborate with it."

---

> **SUBJECT 2 (V.O.)**
> "Utah is weird for tech. It shouldn't work but it does. Maybe that's the point."

---

> **SUBJECT 3 (V.O.)**
> "I'm building something I don't totally understand yet. That used to scare me."

*Beat.*

> **SUBJECT 3 (V.O.) (CONT'D)**
> "Not anymore."

---

## CLOSE (9:00–10:00)

*EXT. UTAH — DUSK*

The landscape again. Different angle. The light is orange now.

A single window glows in a house at the base of a hill.

Someone inside, working.

*Fade to black.*

**TITLE CARD:** *Filmed in Utah, 2026*

*Music fades.*

---

*END*
